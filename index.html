The Future of Naming, Powered by AI

Go from a spark of an idea to the perfect, brandable name in seconds. NameIT is your creative partner for naming anything and everything.

What is NameIT?

NameIT is an intelligent suite of tools designed to streamline your creative process. It's more than just a generator; it's a comprehensive naming and branding assistant. By leveraging advanced AI, we help you brainstorm unique ideas, refine them to perfection, analyze their market potential, and check their availability across the digital landscape—all in one seamless workflow.

Why a Great Name Matters

Your Name is Your First Impression

In a crowded digital world, a name is more than just a label—it's your story, your identity, and your promise to the customer, all distilled into a single word or phrase. A powerful name is memorable, easy to pronounce, and evokes the right emotions. It sets the foundation for your entire brand, influencing logos, marketing, and how people connect with your creation.

From Idea to Identity

Choosing the right name is a critical step that can significantly impact your success. A weak name can be forgettable or confusing, while a strong name can open doors and build instant credibility. NameIT is designed to bridge that gap, transforming your vision into a brand identity that stands out and resonates.

Our Suite of AI Tools

Name Generator

The core of NameIT. Simply provide a description of your idea, add optional keywords, and select a style. Our AI delves into the context to produce a curated list of creative, relevant names, each with a detailed explanation of its meaning and potential.

Custom Refiner

Have a name idea but it's not quite right? Input your existing name and provide instructions. The AI will intelligently generate targeted variations—making it shorter, more professional, adding a mythical twist, or anything else you can imagine.

Availability Checker

A great name is only useful if it's available. Instantly check your top choices across dozens of social media platforms and popular domain extensions (.com, .io, .ai, etc.) to ensure your brand has a unique digital footprint from day one.

Name Analyzer

Go beyond availability with a deep-dive analysis. Our AI provides a brandability score, checks for potential conflicts, and, with our Business Tier, performs an in-depth Audience Persona Analysis to see how your name will resonate with specific demographics and markets.

Text Summarizer

Distill long-form text, product descriptions, or mission statements into their core essence. Generate concise summaries, powerful taglines, or even single keywords to help you focus your naming efforts and marketing message.

Word Combiner

Stuck between a few different ideas? Merge multiple keywords or concepts into a single, unique, and brandable new word. Our AI expertly blends phonetics and meaning to create memorable and innovative portmanteaus.
