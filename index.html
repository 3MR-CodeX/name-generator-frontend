About NameIT

            
The ultimate AI-powered tool for creative naming and branding.

            

                
 Our Story: Why We Built NameIT

                
NameIT was born from a simple, universal struggle: the daunting task of finding the perfect name. As creators, developers, and entrepreneurs ourselves, we’ve spent countless hours brainstorming, only to find our best ideas were already taken or didn't quite capture the essence of our projects. We knew there had to be a better way.

                
We envisioned a tool that wasn’t just a random word generator, but an intelligent partner in the creative process. A platform that could understand the nuance of an idea, explore different creative avenues, and instantly validate the results. NameIT is our solution—an AI-powered muse designed to break through creative blocks and turn the frustration of naming into an inspiring journey of discovery.

Frequently Asked Questions

                    

                        
Are these one-time payments?

                        
Yes. This is not a subscription. You buy a credit pack once and use the credits at your own pace. They never expire.

                    

                    

                        
What can I use credits on?

                        
Credits are used for all AI actions. A basic name generation costs 1 credit per name, while advanced tools like the Name Analyzer cost a few more. Your credit balance is always visible at the top of the page.

                    

                    

                        
What happens when I run out of credits?

                        
You can purchase another top-up pack from the "Buy Credits" page at any time. All your unlocked Pro features will remain active, and you'll also receive 250 free credits every month.
